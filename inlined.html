Hail Fair Travelers to the Castle!

Through the thicket and briar lies a pathway into a forest of the fae. This istheir favorite time of the year, when they ready their charms for mortals. Makeyour way through this enchantment and you will find... a castle!

A New And Exciting Partner Has Joined Our Party!

For adventurers on our First Round of 2022, D&D in a Castle isthrilled to partner with the noble nerds of Nord Games!

Nord Games will gift Round 1 travelers with some incredible free treasures uponarrival at the castle and questers can play a three-day campaign with the best of thebest from Nord Games and those players will receive other exclusive Nord Gamesgifts!

Nord Games is a designer of fun and exciting Board Games, Card Games, andRole-Playing Games (RPGs) focused on producing family-friendly games thatchallenge players and promote a positive atmosphere.

Game with the Nord Games Team!

Nord Games is sending their top talent to the castle to lead you as DMs and to enhanceyour experience with NPC talent for Round 1 (March 26th - 30th). These dates are fast approaching, so time is of the essence, fair adventurers!

Ralph Stickley first got into D&D while atuniversity in 2010, with fellow students onhis acting course; as you can imagine, thegames were quite character-focused! He isthe co-designer and co-writer of NordGames’ first book, Ultimate NPCs:Skulduggery. He has since taken on thelayout and art direction for subsequentprojects, as well as continuing in his role aslead writer on the Ultimate NPCs and Ultimate Bestiary series, along with bitsand pieces of writing and editing on otherbook series and card decks.

Ralph will play 'Rule of Three', a wide-rangingmini-campaign based around undeath andputting right the mistakes of the past!

JVC Parry (Josh) is a freelance TTRPGauthor, and has been publishing materialsince 2016. His work ranges from best-selling adventures on the DMsGuild to self-published indie games, on top of work for3rd party publishers. While JVC Parry isprobably best known for his DMsGuildcampaign Call from the Deep, he’s alsogarnered years of writing experience withcompanies such as Nord Games,LoreSmyth, Jetpack7, and many more.

JVC will play 'Shadow War', a gothic murdermystery with a limited timeframe and risingtensions!

Lou Fryer is part of the British contingentof Nord Games, and the resident grammargeek, mostly managing to be on the caseof every writer in the company from awhole continent away while inwardlysighing at every letter ‘u’ she has to edit outof her own writing (‘aluminum’ and ‘color’,honestly…). Almost all Nord Games’ titlescome through her as an editor, but she isalso the co-writer of the Ultimate NPCs and Ultimate Bestiary series.

Lou will lend her talents to One-Shots and NPCs at the Castle!
